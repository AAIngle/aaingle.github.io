About

Game review websites can be contentious in the gaming space for reductive scores, quick turnarounds, appealing more to algorithms or hype cycles. I think these things distract from the purpose of reviews in the first place.

I made this blog to demonstrate what I think reviews are for -- balancing the subjective and objective, reflecting on how games and opinions change over time, and encouraging evaluations from different perspectives. I hope you enjoy!
